Book of the Month

Editor's Choice

Middlemarch

George Eliot  ·  1871–72

★★★★★

Often regarded as the greatest novel in the English language, Middlemarch weaves the intertwined fates of Dorothea Brooke, Tertius Lydgate, and the citizens of a provincial Midlands town into an unmatched tapestry of human ambition, love, and disillusionment.

"George Eliot's masterpiece stands alone — a novel of moral seriousness, comic grace, and heartbreaking insight into the human condition." — The Times Literary Supplement
